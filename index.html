Blockchain is a technology that has the potential to transform various sectors and contribute to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

By creating decentralized, transparent and secure networks, blockchain can enable innovative solutions for global challenges such as financial inclusion, inequality and climate change.

Some examples of other projects that use blockchain for these purposes are:

  • Hosting a node of a blockchain using renewable energy, which reduces the environmental impact of cryptocurrency mining and supports SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy).
  • financial and cryptocurrency platforms that promote access to credit, transparency in transactions and democratization of economic opportunities, which aligns with SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) and SDG 10 (Reduced Inequalities).
  • blockchain to register renewable energy contracts, which provides more security and trust in the negotiation and verification of these contracts and helps achieve SDG 13 (Climate Action).
